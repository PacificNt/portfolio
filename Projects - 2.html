But prior to doing the prediction, the data is first inspected and then cleaned by dropping unneeded columns and replacing missing values with means, then some exploratory analysis is performed on the data and finaly various data models are evaluated to see which one is the best fit to predict house price.

AirBnB Tableau Project

In this project, I created a Dashboard on AirBnbs in the US. The Dashboard is made of charts on price per zipcode, average price per berdroom, number of listings per number of berdooms, and revenue in a year. This is to help anybody trying to list their property as a AirBnb have a general view on how AirBnbs are doing in the US, which time is the best to list an AirBnB and how much he can expect to make based on the location of the property and the number of bedrooms.
